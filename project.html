SLB OneSubsea

  • Vxuxs Project (TOTAL Energies)

    Role: Lead Product Engineer
    Managed engineering responsibility for 16 foundations, 28 Umbilical Termination Assemblies (UTAs), 4 Flowline Distribution Frames (FLDFs), and 2 Subsea Distribution Units (SDUs). The project is planned for installation in an offshore field in Namibia, with manufacturing, assembly, and testing activities distributed across Malaysia, Norway, and Namibia. Components were sourced globally from Norway, the UK, Malaysia, China, India, the USA, and Brazil. This role required coordination across geographically dispersed stakeholders, supplier interfaces, and cross-cultural engineering teams, ensuring technical compliance, schedule adherence, and supply-chain integration.

  • Roncador Project (Petrobras)

    Role: Lead Product Engineer
    Oversaw engineering delivery for 4 subsea foundations for an offshore field in Brazil. Manufacturing, assembly, and testing were conducted in Brazil, with components procured from the UK, Brazil, and the USA. The project emphasized localization of manufacturing and supplier coordination while maintaining global engineering standards.

  • Product Standardization Initiative (Internal Project)

    Role: Lead Product Engineer
    Led the development of a standardized subsea foundation design, with approximately 80% of components optimized for reuse across multiple offshore fields worldwide. The standardized product was designed to be manufacturable at both internal and external facilities, resulting in significant reductions in cost, lead time, and engineering effort, while improving scalability and repeatability across projects.

  • LAPA Southwest Project (TOTAL Energies)

    Role: Product Engineer
    Managed engineering scope for 4 foundations, 6 UTAs, and 2 SDUs for an offshore field in Brazil. Manufacturing, assembly, and testing were planned across Brazil and the USA, with components sourced from Norway, the UK, China, India, the USA, and Brazil. The project involved complex logistics planning and coordination between multiple international vendors and fabrication yards.
